How to Feed Your Furry Friends Healthily

-- PetsBuddy® shares pet nutrition knowledge with Chinese pet owners in Beijing International Pet Expo 2019

23 Mar 2019 0 comments

In 2019 Beijing International Pet Expo, according to the best arrangement of our Chinese business partner Margo China Inc., Alex Dunne from R&D department of Australia Pet Industry Group gave Chinese pets families a presentation titled ‘How to Rise Your Furry Friends in a Healthy Way’.

In this presentation, Alex briefly explained how the 3 key nutrients, Protein, carbohydrates and fats, to work. These so-called macro-nutrients make up 90% of nutrients in food. And also, other micro-nutritients don’t make up as much of the nutrients in our food, but are absolutely essential. The PetsBuddy® Goatsmilk is a perfect substantial supplement of both macro and micro nutrients to pets. We prepared some gifts for audience to make more people can benefit from PetsBuddy® unique healthy pet food.
