Fresh regional ingredients

We carefully make appropriate foods from fresh regional ingredients. Unmatched by any other dog or cat food, our fresh regional ingredients rival mother nature , supplying nutrients in their freshest, most natural and nourishing form. Our kitchens provide inspiration for our foods and world-class ingredients from Australian farmers and hunters directly.

Our key actives are backed by over 40 years’ experience, and are endorsed by zookeepers, dog owners and veterinarians. We use only the highest-quality-human-grade dairy sourced from Australia’s golden milk belt region. Our materials meet the high standards of GMP, HACCP and SQF. We are proud to provide an authentic and trusted Australian made product, that supports growth in the local industry.

Research and Development

Our commitment to animal health is reflected in the innovation and consistent quality of our products. We start with the most up to date nutritional knowledge backed by research and product testing to create products that are natural and nutritionally balanced. The main methods of testing revolve around palatability and digestibility of our products under controlled and supervised feeding trials, ensuring the continued high quality of our products.

Trusted by pet lovers everywhere, and your trust means everything to us. Our foods and treats are brimming with goodness and guaranteed to keep your cherished dogs and cats happy, healthy and strong.
