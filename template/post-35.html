We Love Pets, We Are Here for Spoil Them

11 Aug 2019 0 comments

With ENVON PET SUPPLIES, the distributor for Sydney market, PetsBuddy® joined in one of the biggest pet shows in Australia—Sydney Dog Lover Show.

During this show, PetsBuddy® products were appreciated by both of professional traders and customers.

Chole, one of PetsBuddy® royal customers, even bought 4 cans of goatsmilk at the venue. She said, I have three dogs, they are always fed with PetsBuddy® formulated goatsmilk, they love to drink and grow faster and stronger, compared with similar dogs at their ages. So I will continue to use PetsBuddy goatsmilk.

Mark, a new customer for PetsBuddy®, ‘I take two bags of freeze dried roo meat cubes for my big labrador as he is under training at a pet school. I know roo meat is very healthy and freeze dried treats can keep more nutritions, this is why I take them immediatedly once I find in this show.

For charity organisations, PetsBuddy® has the passion to offer them as much as we can. During this expo, Australia Pet Industry Group donated products contains formulated goatsmilk and healthy roo treats to such organisation and will continue to support them in the future.
