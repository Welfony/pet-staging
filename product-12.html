Formulated Guinea Pig Replacement 210g

This high protein milk supplement is able to sustain the rapid growth phase of a guinea pig in the first three weeks of life. This formula also contains stabilised Vitamin C, an essential nutrient for pups, that helps prevent scurvy, which can cause problems with skin, joints and blood clotting.

  • Storage

    Once the Formulated Guinea Pig Replacement powder has been mixed with water (according to the directions), the milk can be stored in the fridge for 1 day or frozen for up to 1 week.

    Once the tin has been opened, the rest of the powder is only safe to use for 28 days. Note the date of first opening the tin on the lid. Discard remaining powder if not used within 28 days.

  • Directions for use

    Mix 5g (1 level scoop) of PetsBuddy® FORMULATED GUINEA PIG REPLACEMENT with 30ml of pre-boiled warm water, to achieve a total volume of 32ml. initially feed from a syringe or bottle and eat. Pups are born well-developed and may be encouraged to lap from an early age. Feed every 2-3 hours for the first 5 days, reducing this to every 4-6 hours as pups get older. Toilet pups manually during the first week of life. Dehydration, always provide access to safe drinking water. Birth weight is usually around 60-110g. body weight may be stable for the first couple of days, but should increase by around 3-7g per day thereafter. Weigh pups regularly to verify growth rate and determine feed volumes. Overfeeding can cause diarrhoea, so feed the suggested amounts in the feeding guide. Pups may begin to show interest in solid food at around 2-3 days of age. Establish young guinea pigs on hay, green leafy vegetables and some pallets. Continue to increase solids and reduce milk intake until they are fully weaned at around 21 days.

    *If necessary, please consult your veterinarian for additional advice.
