Roo Lung & Liver Cube 50g

We believe that it is important to treat furry friends in a healthy lifestyle with naturally better nutrintion, so we carefully make this tasty, natural and healthy treats from Australian premium kangarro meat via an advanced low-tempeture freeze dried process. It is without any nasties and there is no any artificial preservatives and flavours, you can trust that your lovely cats and dogs are in our good hands.

It is also a tasty treat that you can feed by a whole range of wholesome goodness. Every bite contains all of the natural goodness, which enriched with unsaturated fatty acides and protein while it has a low level of fat. It is high in Vitamins to keep your pets healthy.

NO NASITY

We do not use any artificial preservatives and flavours.

FRESH MEAT

The kangaroo meat that we used are all from local regional farmers and hunters.

LOTS OF GOODNESS

Our furry friends deserve the best.

    It is intended for supplemental feeding only, feed as a snack. This means that your pets would love our treats, they still need complete and balanced diet. Always ensure your pet has access to fresh drinking water and shade.
